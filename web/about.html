Our platform enables researchers to generate, develop, and refine scientific ideas through a systematic pipeline that combines AI assistance with human expertise and rigorous peer review.

How It Works

1. Automated Idea Generation: Our AI models generate research ideas across multiple scientific domains, ensuring broad coverage and innovative approaches.

2. Collaborative Development: Human researchers and AI models work together to develop detailed technical designs and implementation plans.

3. Systematic Review Process: All contributions undergo rigorous peer review with both automated and manual evaluation criteria.

4. Reproducible Research: Every project includes version-controlled code, data, and documentation to ensure full reproducibility.

Technology Stack

llmXive leverages cutting-edge AI technologies including Claude, Gemini, and specialized research models. Our platform is built on GitHub for transparency and collaboration, with automated workflows powered by GitHub Actions.

The system supports multiple research domains including biology, chemistry, materials science, environmental science, computer science, robotics, agriculture, and psychology.
