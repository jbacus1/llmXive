## Introduction
You are being invited to participate in a research study. Please read this form carefully and ask questions before signing.

## Purpose of the Study
This study aims to evaluate the effectiveness of a mindfulness-based intervention on social skills development in children aged 6-12 with ASD. We expect to enroll approximately 60 participants across a 12-week intervention period.

## Procedures
If you consent to your child's participation, they will:
- Complete baseline assessments (approximately 2 hours)
- Participate in 12 weekly 45-minute mindfulness sessions
- Complete follow-up assessments at 3 months and 6 months post-intervention
- Provide parent-reported outcome measures at each timepoint

## Time Commitment
- Baseline: 2 hours
- Intervention: 12 sessions × 45 minutes = 9 hours
- Follow-up assessments: 2 hours each (2 timepoints)
- Total: Approximately 14 hours over 6 months

## Risks and Discomforts
- Minimal risk: Some children may find mindfulness exercises initially uncomfortable
- Potential emotional responses during discussions about social challenges
- Time burden for family participation
- All concerns can be discussed with the research team

## Benefits
- Direct: Your child may develop improved mindfulness and social skills
- Indirect: Contribution to scientific understanding of ASD interventions
- No guarantee of individual benefit

## Confidentiality
- All data will be de-identified using unique participant codes
- Protected health information (PHI) stored on encrypted, password-protected systems
- Data retained for 7 years per IRB requirements
- Only the research team will have access to identifiable information
- Results will be published in aggregate form only

## HIPAA Authorization
You authorize the research team to collect, use, and disclose your child's protected health information as necessary for this study. You may revoke this authorization in writing at any time.

## Voluntary Participation
Participation is completely voluntary. You may:
- Refuse to participate without penalty
- Withdraw at any time without affecting your child's medical care
- Skip any questions or activities they find uncomfortable

## Costs and Compensation
- No cost to participate
- Compensation: $25 gift card per assessment visit (total $75)
- Transportation assistance available if needed
