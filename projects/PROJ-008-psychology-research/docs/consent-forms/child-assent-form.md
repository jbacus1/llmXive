# Child Assent Form

## What We're Asking You to Do
Hi! We're doing a study about mindfulness and social skills for kids like you. We want to know if our program helps kids feel calmer and make friends easier.

## What Happens If You Join?
If you say yes to join, here's what you'll do:
- Meet with us a few times to answer some questions and do some activities
- Come to 12 group sessions where we'll learn mindfulness games together
- Do some activities again a few months later to see how things are going

## How Long Will It Take?
- First meeting: About 2 hours (you can take breaks!)
- Each session: 45 minutes, once a week for 12 weeks
- Later meetings: About 2 hours each, twice more

## What If You Don't Want to Join?
That's okay! You don't have to join if you don't want to. Your parents and doctors will still take good care of you.

## Can You Quit If You Change Your Mind?
Yes! You can stop anytime you want. Just tell us or your parents. You won't get in trouble.

## What's Good About Joining?
- You'll learn some cool relaxation tricks
- You'll get to meet other kids
- You might feel better at school and with friends
- You'll get a small gift for each time you come back

## What Could Be Hard About Joining?
- Some questions might feel tricky to answer
- Some activities might feel a little uncomfortable at first
- It takes time to come to the sessions
- If anything bothers you, tell us right away

## Will Anyone Know It's You?
No! We'll use a special code instead of your name when we write about the study. Only the researchers will know who you are.

## What If You Have Questions?
Ask your parents, or call us:
- [Researcher Name]: [phone]
- Your parents can also call if they have questions
