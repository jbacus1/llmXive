# Content Revision Prompt

The following {review_type} received a score of {score:.2f} and needs improvement.

**Original content:**
{current_content}

**Reviewer feedback:**
{feedback}

## Instructions
Please revise the content to address all concerns and improve quality. Maintain the same format but enhance the content based on the feedback.

## Variables
- {review_type}: The type of content being revised
- {score}: The review score received (formatted to 2 decimal places)
- {current_content}: The original content that needs revision
- {feedback}: The reviewer's feedback and recommendations
