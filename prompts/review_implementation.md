## Review Criteria
Evaluate the plan on:
1. Task breakdown completeness (1-10 scale)
2. Timeline realism (1-10 scale)
3. Resource allocation (1-10 scale)
4. Risk assessment quality (1-10 scale)
5. Success criteria clarity (1-10 scale)

## Review Structure Required
**SCORE: [overall score 1-10]**

**DETAILED FEEDBACK:**
- Strengths: [list key strengths]
- Weaknesses: [list major weaknesses]
- Missing elements: [what's missing]
- Recommendations: [specific improvements]

**VERDICT:** [ACCEPT/REVISE/REJECT]

## Variables
- {content}: The implementation plan content to review
